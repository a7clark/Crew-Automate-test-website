// FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to know how to code to join? +
Not at all. Crew Automate welcomes builders at every level — from no-coders using Make and Zapier to engineers writing custom Python scripts. The community has resources for everyone, and members are happy to help regardless of where you're starting from.
What tools does the community focus on? +
We cover the full stack — Make.com, n8n, Zapier, OpenAI, Claude, Airtable, Notion, Python, Google Sheets, HubSpot, and more. If it can be automated, someone in the community has probably built it and is happy to share.
What is the WAT framework? +
WAT stands for Workflows, Agents, Tools — a structured framework for building reliable AI-powered automation systems. Workflows define what to do, Agents handle intelligent decision-making, and Tools execute the work. It's the backbone of how Crew Automate members build and share systems.
Can I cancel my Pro membership anytime? +
Yes. Pro and Agency memberships are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime and you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
How active is the community? +
Very. Members post daily, questions typically get answered within hours, and we host live sessions and AMAs regularly. The community is built for practitioners — people who are actively building — so the signal-to-noise ratio is high.
